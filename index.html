Philosophy

Most Businesses Don't Have
a People Problem.
They Have a Systems Problem.

Work gets buried in coordination, handoffs, and invisible friction. The role of systems is to remove work that should not exist in the first place.

Systems aren't there to replace people. They remove the work that should not exist in the first place.

When systems are right, people stop doing pointless work and focus on what actually matters.
